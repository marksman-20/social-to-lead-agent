# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## Pricing & Features

AutoStream offers automated video editing tools for content creators. We currently have two main subscription plans:

### Basic Plan
- **Price:** $29/month
- **Video Limit:** 10 videos/month
- **Resolution:** 720p resolution

### Pro Plan
- **Price:** $79/month
- **Video Limit:** Unlimited videos
- **Resolution:** 4K resolution
- **Features:** AI captions included

## Company Policies

- **Refund Policy:** No refunds are issued after 7 days of purchase or renewal.
- **Customer Support:** 24/7 support is available, but this is exclusively available for users on the Pro plan. Basic plan users have access to email support with a 48-hour SLA.
